ASSERT condition
Evaluates numeric expression condition and stops, if it equals zero, reporting "V ASSERT failed" If the expression does not equal zero, it has no effects beyond those of evaluating the expression. Useful for debugging, asserting invariant conditions to find out when they do not hold.
